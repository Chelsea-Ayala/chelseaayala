Grooming Stations

Is your tub too small to properly wash your dog. Do groomers make grooming fees out of your price range. Renting a space at Doggie Suds might be just what you need. We offer spacious stations with grooming-friendly equipment. These stations give you the space and equipment you need to wash your pet properly and safely. For your added convenience you can either purchase grooming products on site or bring your own. Bathing tubs are available by appointment or walk in, however appointments will take precedent. Proof of rabies vaccination is required.
